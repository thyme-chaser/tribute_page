Singer, song writer, acter: GACKT
  • Birth name: Gakuto Oshiro
  • Also known as Gackt Camui
  • Born July 4, 1973 in Okinawa, Japan
  • At a young age, learned piano and classical music
  • Became insterested in rock music in high school
  • 1993: became the frontman for short-lived band Cains:Feel
  • After Cains:Feel, Gackt joined the visual kei band Malice Mizer
  • 1999: Goes solo
  • Gackt has released nine studio albums, and with 48 singles, holds the male soloist record for most consecutive singles in Japanese music history
  • His single "Returner(Yami no Shuen)", released on June 20, 2007 was his first and only single to hit number one on the Orion Charts
  • Gackt has sold over 10 million records as a solo artist
  • His music has also been used for video games, anime and tv shows
  • He has also acted in a few films, including his international debut, Bunraku
  • Gackt has also performed with the Tokyo Philharmonic Orchestra twice
